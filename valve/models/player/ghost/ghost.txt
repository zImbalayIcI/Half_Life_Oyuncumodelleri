August 2, 2011  Ghost Skin by Demasturbot


The model is transparent, set brightness to 0.7 for better perfomance.
Feel free to contact me: valerique9@gmail.com


INSTALL:
Place ghost.mdl, ghost.bmp and ghost.txt in a new folder
called ghost in valve\models\player folder in your Half-Life
directory.


CREDITS:
The initial mesh was taken from the Ghost Controller (HL monster)
model by some unknown author.
